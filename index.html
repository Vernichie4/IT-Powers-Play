How IT Transformed Play

1972

Pong launches the video game revolution, introducing simple yet addictive digital entertainment. This marks the beginning of IT's crucial role in gaming.

1990s

The rise of the internet revolutionizes gaming through LAN parties and early online multiplayer. Games like DOOM and Quake showcase networking's potential in entertainment.

2007

The iPhone debuts, transforming phones into gaming devices. Mobile development explodes, making games accessible to billions and creating new career opportunities.

2020s

AI and Virtual Reality reshape gaming experiences. Machine learning powers NPCs, procedural generation creates infinite worlds, and VR delivers unprecedented immersion.
